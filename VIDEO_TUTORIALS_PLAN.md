## Production Guidelines

### Video Format
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate:** 30 fps
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Audio:** Clear narration, no background music

### Recording Setup
- **Screen Recording:** OBS Studio or Camtasia
- **Microphone:** Good quality USB mic
- **Environment:** Quiet room
- **Script:** Prepared outline

### Editing Guidelines
- Add intro/outro (5 seconds each)
- Include chapter markers
- Add captions/subtitles
- Highlight cursor for visibility
- Zoom in on important details
- Cut out mistakes and pauses

### Branding
- Company logo in corner
- Consistent color scheme
- Professional intro animation
- Contact information in description
